This Is The Ingleburn RSL

Ingleburn RSL

Ingleburn RSL Club, located on 70 Chester Road in Ingleburn, NSW, has been a cornerstone of the local community since its founding in 1926. Its mission is to connect with and support the community, while honouring and serving those who have served with us, and before us. The club offers a variety of facilities, including the Salute Restaurant, Poppy Café, Rick Madden Lounge, and Crystal & Sports Bars. The Salute Restaurant serves lunch and dinner daily, catering to meat lovers, vegetarians, and those requiring gluten-free options, with a menu featuring starters, snacks, salads, family favourites, and value meals. Our Poppy Café provides a selection of savoury options like pizza, sandwiches, wraps, pies, and salads, along with desserts and beverages. The club is always hosting entertaining events everyday, and weekly Bingo games, suitable for everyone. Come join and becom a part of us.
