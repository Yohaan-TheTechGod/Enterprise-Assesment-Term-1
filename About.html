About Us

Ingleburn RSL Club has a proud history rooted in community and service. Established after World War II to support returning servicemen and women, the club quickly became a central hub for veterans and local residents alike. Over the decades, it has grown from a modest meeting place into a vibrant community venue offering dining, entertainment, and social support services. Ingleburn RSL remains dedicated to honoring Australia’s military history while actively contributing to the well-being of current and former service members.
