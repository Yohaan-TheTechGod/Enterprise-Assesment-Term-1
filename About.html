This is the Ingleburn RSL club

A Sub branch of RSL New South Wales, we were established in 1926.

Our goal is to honour and serve those who have serverd our nation, both past and present.

We aim to collaborate with the community of Ingleburn and the surrounding South-Western Sydney suburbs, in order to create a community that shares the passion of the Australian Defense Force, and aims to appreciate and support veterans of Australian Commonwealth forces.

The RSL hosts multiple events dialy and weekly, giving patrons access to a variety of Entertainment and activities.

RSL Ingleburn also has catering for corprate and special events.

We hope that you can join us on our special events, such as our esteemed yearly ANZAC day commenoration on the 25th of April every year.

Become a memeber and join our groing community, full of people from all ages of this nations history.

Or if you would like to, donate to help fund our cause, helping veterans and RSL operations in Australia and internationally with our deployed men overseas.

Come join us, and be a part of something great in your community.
